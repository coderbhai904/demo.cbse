Academic Services

Curriculum Design and Implementation: CBSE prescribes syllabi for various classes (Class I to XII) and ensures their implementation. Affiliation of Schools: CBSE grants affiliation to schools across India and abroad, ensuring they meet its standards. Examinations: Conducts board examinations for Class X and XII and entrance exams like the JEE (Main) for engineering and NEET for medical education. Assessment and Evaluation: Offers grading systems and assessments, including continuous and comprehensive evaluation (CCE).
